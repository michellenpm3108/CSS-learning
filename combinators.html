  • combinators
  1. intro p : It will select all paragraphs that are DECENDANT of .intro.
  2. intro > p : It will only select paragraphs if they are DIRECT CHILD of .intro, but not if they are nested inside of something else.
  3. h1 + p : It will only apply to paragraphs that are RIGH AFTER an h1.
  4. h1 ~ p : It will select all paragraphs that are siblings to the h1.
